How it works

You get 2 teams*
Randomly assigned. You didn't pick them. That's not your fault. Own it.
Find one fact
About either of your countries. Geography, food, music — not required to involve a ball.
Weekly trivia
One question per week. Earns points. Still doesn't require watching football.
Collect points
Your teams score goals. You score points. Somehow everyone wins.

 

* Depending on participant numbers, you may be assigned 2 or 3 teams.
